Who is Onyx

Onyx is an open source voice assistant. It can be used everywhere and help you. It is open so it can be remixed, improved...

OPEN SOURCE

Onyx is an open platform that can be extended and expanded. Its only limit is your imagination. It's an Artificial Intelligence just for you !

AMAZING FEATURES

You go home, Onyx lights up the heating and the light and in the morning it can prepare your coffee. It is with its intelligent scenario system that it is able to deduce your facts and act accordingly! You can teach him things as much as he can!

COMMUNITY

The great strength of Onyx is its community! It makes it possible to contribute to the development of Onyx and to improve it every day. You can simply contribute and develop your first plugins and skills in less than 15 minutes.

Onyx Possibilities

Here are some compatibilities available with Onyx

Lights

Onyx lights your lamps according to your needs, to have a wake up in color!

Transports

Onyx gives you the best way to go to your job.

Social Networks

You have difficulties to manage your different social networks? Onyx is there to you!

Mails

You have several email addresses and you do not know how to read everything? Onyx offers you an alternative.

Music

Listen to your favorite music through your best speakers.

Weather

What the weather like? How should I dress? Onyx is there for that!

Alarm

Do you need a fully configurable alarm clock? Who calculates your travel time and wakes you up accordingly?

Calendar

Manage your different meeting through the Onyx calendar.
